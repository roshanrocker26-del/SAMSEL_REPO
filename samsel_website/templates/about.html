Who We Are

Crafting Tomorrow’s Coders with Unwavering Quality since 2001. We empower schools with digital-first resources.

Our Mission

SAMSEL PUBLICATIONS, a unit of USS Unique Software Solutions, Chennai, is a reputed educational publishing house. For over two decades, we have been at the forefront of delivering high-quality, curriculum-aligned academic resources to schools across India.

We believe that education should evolve with technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional learning and modern technological demands, providing students with the tools they need to thrive in a digital world.

What We Offer

Our comprehensive catalogue is continually updated to reflect the latest in technology and educational standards, including NEP 2020. We offer a rich ecosystem of physical and digital learning materials.

  • 50+ Titles across 9 specialized series
  • Covering AI, Robotics, Cyber Security, & Coding
  • QR-linked Interactive Practical Video Demonstrations
  • Teacher Support Manuals & Evaluation Tools
  • Strict alignment with CBSE, ICSE & State Boards
