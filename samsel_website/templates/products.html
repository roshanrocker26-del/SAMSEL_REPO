IBOT SERIES

This book is designed to introduce Class 1 students to the exciting world of computers in a fun and simple way. It begins with understanding smart machines and the basic parts of a computer, helping children recognize how technology helps us in daily life. The book gradually introduces young learners to ScratchJr, where they can start creating small animations and stories through easy blocks.
